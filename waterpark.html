Welcome to Your Water Paradise

The South Water Park, established in 2019, is Jaipur's leading water entertainment destination located in the heart of Sitapura Industrial Area. Our state-of-the-art facility spans over 2 acres and features the latest water technology with international safety standards. We're proud to be Rajasthan's first water park with eco-friendly water treatment systems, ensuring both fun and environmental responsibility.

Our park features 12+ exciting water attractions including high-speed slides, wave pools, lazy rivers, and dedicated kids' zones. With a capacity of 1,500 visitors per day, we maintain excellent staff-to-guest ratios with 50+ trained lifeguards and support staff. Our modern filtration system recycles 100% of our water, making us one of India's most sustainable water parks.
